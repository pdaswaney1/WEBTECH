The History of Jewellery


Across the globe, since man is known to exist, jewelery has been a symbol of wealth and propserity. It is the elegant representation of joy and beauty. Any and every culture, religion, and tradition, is incomplete without the element of decoration. It is the element that unifies and glorifies what we stand for; promoting equality; celebrating differences; displaying cultures; eradicating hate; encouraging love...

A look into the Modern Era


Modern Jewelery market is dominated by the work of gold and silver. The era today distinguishes luxury by delving deeper into handcrafted skills of jewelry making. This eliminates simple gold and stone work to signify wealth and prosperity. The eloquent designs and aethetic beauty of an ornament comes from skillful labour and time dedication. It is simple. The world wants more. The world needs a blend of all global artforms melting into one unique ornament. This need became the inception point for Diamond and Kundan-Meena Jewels. This skillful niche is the blend of all metals, stones, and techniques that were used from the ancient times to the modern.
